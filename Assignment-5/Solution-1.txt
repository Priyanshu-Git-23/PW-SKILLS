Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving the visibility of the website or webpage on search engine result page so as to make a company's website more discoverable, thereby driving traffic and sales.

Importance of SEO are:-
     1. SEO increases the organic search web traffic
     2. Good website SEO leads to a better user experience
     3. SEO results in more sales locally
     4. Website credibility increases with SEO
     5. SEO requires comparatively less constant engagement
